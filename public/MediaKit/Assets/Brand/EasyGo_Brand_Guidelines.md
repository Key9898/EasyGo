# EasyGo Brand Guidelines

## 1. Our Mission
To provide seamless, premium, and reliable car rental experiences for every journey in Thailand.

## 2. Our Voice
- **Professional**: We are experts in fleet management.
- **Friendly**: We are approachable and helpful.
- **Reliable**: We deliver on our promises.

## 3. Visual Identity

### Primary Colors
- **EasyGo Orange**: #EA580C (Vibrant, Energetic)
- **Slate Dark**: #0F172A (Professional, Solid)
- **Pure White**: #FFFFFF (Clean, Modern)

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Clean, Modern Sans-serif)
- **Headings**: Bold, Slate 900
- **Body Text**: Regular, Slate 600

### Logo Usage
- Use the **Full Logo** on light backgrounds.
- Use the **Icon** for avatars, favicons, or mobile headers.
- Do not stretch, rotate, or recolor the logo.

## 4. Imagery
- Use high-resolution photos.
- Focus on "The Journey" and "Freedom".
- Use warm lighting and natural colors.
