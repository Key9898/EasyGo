## About EasyGo
EasyGo is Thailand's premier digital car rental service, offering a seamless booking experience and a fleet of top-tier vehicles ranging from eco-friendly city cars to luxury SUVs and vans. We combine technology with exceptional customer service to make every journey effortless.

## Key Statistics
- **Fleet Size:** 50+ Premium Vehicles including EVs, SUVs, and Sedans.
- **Service Areas:** Bangkok, Suvarnabhumi Airport, Don Mueang Airport, Chiang Mai, Phuket.
- **Customers Served:** 10,000+ Happy Travelers.

## Services
1. **Daily & Weekly Rentals**: Flexible options for tourists and locals.
2. **Corporate Fleet**: Long-term leasing solutions for businesses.

4. **Subscription**: Monthly car subscription with zero maintenance.
